<p><strong>Insights</strong><br>
The bigger the goals, the bigger the results.<br>
I realize that the imagination is unlocking more and more as I see what's possible.<br>
Sometimes I make requests as absurd as a child's wishes, and the AI just handles them.<br>
Design is always the most time-consuming thing for me.<br>
Elon Musk always emphasizes the importance of a tight feedback loop, and he is absolutely right. I show my progress to the AI, get its criticism, and improve it further in each cycle.<br>
The first 99% of the project takes 99% of the time, but the remaining 1% takes another 99%. It's just as difficult.<br>
