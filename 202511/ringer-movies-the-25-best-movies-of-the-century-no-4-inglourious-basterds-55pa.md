<p>The 25 Best Movies of the Century series continues with Quentin Tarantino’s Inglourious Basterds snagging the No. 4 spot. Hosts Sean Fennessey and Amanda Dobbins rave about its hallucinatory WWII take, explain why it edges out Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, and hail Christoph Waltz’s scene-stealing, career-defining performance.</p>

<p>They also dig into the film’s lasting impact—from Tarantino’s razor-sharp dialogue to its bold alternate-history bravado—and why Basterds still ranks as one of the century’s ultimate movie-going experiences.</p>
