<p>Predators (2010) revives the franchise by ditching the Alien vs Predator detours and tossing a rag-tag squad into a muddy jungle deathmatch. It even sneaks in fresh twists that finally make it stand out from the previous, lackluster sequels.</p>

<p>In his Caravan of Garbage review, Mr Sunday Movies hails it as a hidden gem—and can’t help but lament the fact we never got a proper follow-up.</p>
