<p><strong>Sean and Amanda</strong> dive into Greta Gerwig’s <em>Lady Bird</em>, ranking it No. 5 on their list of the 25 Best Movies of the 21st Century. They praise it as the ultimate millennial coming-of-age film, exploring its honest, complicated mother-daughter dynamics and why it stands out over Gerwig’s other hits like <em>Little Women</em> and <em>Frances Ha</em>.</p>

<p>Along the way, they break down what makes <em>Lady Bird</em> so uniquely defining—its perfect blend of humor, heart, and generational truth—and why it earned its spot as their official Gerwig pick. Toss in some State Farm sponsorship love, and you’ve got another Ringer podcast episode worth your time.</p>
