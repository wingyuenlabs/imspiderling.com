<h1>
  
  
  Nano Banana 2 - 4K AI Image Generation Platform
</h1>

<p>## Overview<br>
  Nano Banana 2 is Google's next-generation 4K AI image generation and editing platform powered by Gemini technology. Unlike traditional AI models limited by resolution and accuracy, Nano Banana 2 delivers professional-grade 4K output with revolutionary self-correction workflow.</p>

<p>## Core Features</p>

<p>### 4K Quality Output</p>

<ul>
<li>Native 2K rendering with 4K upscaling</li>
<li>Professional-grade images for print and digital media</li>
<li>Sharper fidelity ideal for commercial applications</li>
</ul>

<p>### Self-Correction Workflow</p>

<ul>
<li>Multi-step AI process: plan → generate → analyze → fix → finalize</li>
<li>Automatically detects and corrects mistakes</li>
<li>Error-free results without manual iterations</li>
</ul>

<p>### Multi-Image Understanding</p>

<ul>
<li>Cross-image context analysis</li>
<li>Coherent edits across multiple related images</li>
<li>Understands relationships and visual narratives</li>
</ul>

<p>### Cultural Context Awareness</p>

<ul>

<li>Authentic regional visual details</li>
<li>Realistic cultural representations</li>
</ul>

<p>### Lightning-Fast Processing</p>

</ul>
