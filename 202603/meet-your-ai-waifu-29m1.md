I just made an AI companion you can talk to on your iPhone/iPad</p>

<p>And it's OPEN-SOURCE!</p>

<p>I built it in 3 nights because the idea wouldn't let me sleep.</p>

<p>She has a 3D anime avatar (sourced from amica-arbius-MIT licensed), talks hands-free, sees through your camera, and remembers everything about you.</p>

<p>How it works:<br>
  → Talk to her naturally, hands-free<br>
  → She sees what you see through your camera<br>
  → She remembers details across every conversation<br>
  → Choose your AI: Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, or others<br>
  → ElevenLabs voice makes her sound real</p>

<p>Your phone is the companion. Your API key is the brain.</p>

<p>I built this because every AI chat app felt lifeless. I wanted something that feels like talking to someone who actually knows you.</p>
