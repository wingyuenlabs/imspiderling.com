<p>You know this loop:</p>

<ol>
<li>Find something online you want to ask AI about</li>
<li>Select all → Copy</li>
<li>Open new tab → ChatGPT</li>
<li>Paste → Ask question</li>
<li>Switch back</li>
</ol>

<p>I did this hundreds of times before I finally built the fix.</p>

<p><strong>Prompt Helix</strong> is a Chrome extension that puts an AI assistant <br>
directly on whatever page you're reading. Click the extension, <br>
ask your question or pick a built-in intent (Summary, ELI5, <br>
Fun Fact), and it sends the page content to your AI of choice <br>
and answers right there.</p>

<h2>
  
  
  What makes it different
</h2>

<ul>
<li>Works with OpenAI, Claude, Gemini and Mistral</li>
<li>You bring your own API key — stays local in your browser</li>
<li>Free tier with no daily caps</li>
<li>Hallucination prevention and confidence scoring built in</li>
</ul>

<h2>
  
  
  The build
</h2>

<p>Built this solo — Chrome extension plus full SaaS backend, <br>
auth system, subscription management, and website <br>
(helixlabs.studio). Few months of evenings.</p>

<p>Already getting organic installs from Chrome Store search <br>
