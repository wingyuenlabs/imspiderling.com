you'll eventually deal with CPM. Here's everything you need to know.</p>

<h2>
  
  
  What is CPM?
</h2>

<p>CPM stands for <strong>Cost Per Mille</strong> — the cost advertisers pay <br>
per 1,000 ad impressions. It's the backbone metric of digital advertising.</p>

<p><strong>CPM Formula:</strong><br>
CPM = (Total Cost / Total Impressions) × 1,000</p>

<h2>
  
  
  Quick Example
</h2>

<p>You spend $500 on a campaign → 200,000 impressions.</p>

<p>CPM = ($500 / 200,000) × 1,000 = <strong>$2.50 CPM</strong></p>

<h2>
  
  
  CPM vs CPC — Which Should You Use?
</h2>

<ul>
<li>
<strong>CPM</strong> → brand awareness, video ads, reach</li>
<li>
<strong>CPC</strong> → clicks, leads, direct sales</li>
</ul>
