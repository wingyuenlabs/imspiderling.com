This one seems to optimize for output.<br>
The Kadena Dev Challenge is live, and it’s centered on one thing:<br>
getting developers to actually ship.<br>
If you’ve been exploring:<br>
• Smart contract development<br>
• Blockchain tooling<br>
• dApp ideas you haven’t executed yet<br>
• New ecosystems beyond the usual<br>

What you’re expected to do is simple:<br>
• Build on Kadena<br>
• Use the Pact language<br>

You go from “idea phase” to something real you can demo.<br>
And that matters more than any tutorial ever will.<br>
Even without rewards, you gain:<br>
