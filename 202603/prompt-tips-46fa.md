<p>Ask ChatGPT to help you create tables with the most important aspects of a big text. It’s better than bullet points, imo (when the text is really big). You can ask for some refinements like small definitions of the most important concepts, or wikipedia links, too (or your favorite website). You can also use PowerAutomate to interact with your browser and automate some of these commands.</p>
